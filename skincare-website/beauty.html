Beauty Tips & Routines

Enhance your natural beauty with expert advice and techniques.

A.M. Routine

A morning routine that protects your skin from pollution, UV rays, and excess oil. Steps to include: Cleanse your face with a gentle face wash. Apply a light, oil-free moisturizer. Always use sunscreen (SPF 30 or higher). Use an SPF lip balm to keep lips soft. Benefits: Keeps your skin fresh, hydrated, and protected throughout the day.

P.M. Routines

Night time is when your skin repairs and rejuvenates itself. Steps to include: Remove makeup and cleanse your face. Apply toner or rose water to tighten pores. Use a nourishing serum (like Vitamin C or Retinol). Finish with a night cream or aloe vera gel. Benefits: Repairs damage, restores glow, and promotes healthy skin overnight.

Weekly Routine

Skincare Routine

A deep cleansing routine done once or twice a week for extra care. Steps to include: Exfoliate with a mild scrub to remove dead skin. Apply a hydrating or clay mask based on your skin type. Steam your face for a few minutes, then rinse with cold water. Finish with a moisturizer or sheet mask. Benefits: Unclogs pores, refreshes skin, and maintains smooth texture..

Seasonal Care

Your skin needs change with the weather — adjust your routine accordingly. Steps to include: Summer: Use gel-based moisturizers and sunscreen. Winter: Use rich creams and stay hydrated. Monsoon: Keep skin dry and clean; use antibacterial toner. Spring/Autumn: Gentle exfoliation and light hydration.
